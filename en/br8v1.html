Wireless 8 channels biopotential measurement system «BR8V1»

Based on Texas Instruments Texas Instruments ADS1298 Analog Front-End - one of the best solutions for EEG/ECG measurements.

  • 8 independent differential channels
  • 24 bit resolution ADC
  • Adjustable sampling rate up to 1 kHz
  • Adjustable input range up to 1200 mV
  • Power consumption 35mA, 3.7V
  • BDF / EDF data format
  • Wireless (Bluetooth computer interface)
  • Lead off detection
  • Noise level 4 mkV peak-to-peak for a bandwidth of 150 Hz
  • Build in 3 channel accelerometer
